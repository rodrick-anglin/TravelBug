TravelBug

Your travel. Automatically tracked and visualized.
TravelBug turns your flights, trips, and locations into a personal travel dashboard. Track your history, visualize your journey, and unlock insights like miles flown, countries visited, and travel patterns.
  • Import flights from your calendar
  • Track countries, cities, and trips
  • View travel stats and insights
  • Visualize routes on a world map
  • Add notes and memories
  • Share with friend!
