TravelBug

Your travel. Automatically tracked and visualized.
TravelBug turns your flights, trips, and locations into a personal travel dashboard. Track your history, visualize your journey on a world map, and unlock insights like miles flown, countries visited, and travel patterns.
Import flights automatically from your calendar
Track countries, cities, and trips
View travel stats and insights
Visualize routes on an interactive map
Add notes and memories to trips

Google Calendar Integration

TravelBug can optionally connect to your Google Calendar to identify and import travel-related events such as flights. This allows the app to automatically build your travel history and generate insights.

This data is processed only on your device and is never stored on external servers or shared with third parties. You can choose whether or not to grant access and can revoke access at any time from your device settings.
