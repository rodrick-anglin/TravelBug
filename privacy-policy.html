TravelBug - Around The World Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

TravelBug - Around The World respects your privacy.

The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers.

All travel data such as places visited, flights, routes, notes, and statistics are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to share travel information using iOS sharing features, that information is shared only with the services you select.

Map functionality uses Apple Maps and may be subject to Apple's privacy policy.
